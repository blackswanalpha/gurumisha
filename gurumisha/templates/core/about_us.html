ABOUT GURUMISHA

Kenya's premier automotive marketplace, connecting car enthusiasts with their dream vehicles and providing world-class import services since our inception.

Our Story

Founded with a vision to revolutionize the automotive marketplace in Kenya, Gurumisha Motors has grown to become the most trusted platform for buying, selling, and importing vehicles across East Africa.

We understand that purchasing a vehicle is one of life's most significant investments. That's why we've built a platform that prioritizes transparency, quality, and customer satisfaction above all else.

Our team of automotive experts works tirelessly to ensure every vehicle listed on our platform meets the highest standards of quality and reliability. From local dealers to international imports, we maintain strict quality controls.

Today, we're proud to be Kenya's leading automotive marketplace, facilitating thousands of successful transactions and helping families find their perfect vehicles.
